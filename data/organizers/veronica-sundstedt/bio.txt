Prof. in Computer Science at BTH. Her research focuses on human-centered visual and interactive computing, contributing to interdisciplinary research at the intersection of computer graphics, visualization, HCI, and AI. She is leading the HINTS research project and has expertise in human factors/user studies relevant to advancing human-centered approaches in intelligent immersive environments. She has organized courses at SIGGRAPH/SIGGRAPH ASIA and workshops at SIGCHI/CHI PLAY.
